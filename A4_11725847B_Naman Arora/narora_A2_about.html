My latest project involves developing an AI-based model that not only detects objects within images but also allows users to interact with the screen through color-based gestures. It's a step towards more intuitive and accessible technology.

My technical toolkit is powered by a strong command over languages like Python and Java, with advanced proficiency that fuels my software development endeavors. I am proud of my certifications in C, C++, and JavaScript, which further cement my foundational coding skills.

Beyond my specialized knowledge, I possess a solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, database management, and web development frameworks that are essential for any programmer. With an analytical mindset and a knack for problem-solving, I am always ready to tackle challenges in coding and software design.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me contributing to open-source projects, participating in hackathons, and staying abreast of the latest tech trends. I believe in lifelong learning and continuously seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills.
